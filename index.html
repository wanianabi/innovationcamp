How can we help children in underdeveloped countries to obtain an education through motivating them to stay in school?

How can we help them shape a future?

How can we make education interesting and motivating?

There are 130 million children across the world who are in school yet has not learned how to read, write or do math. What this shows is that the teachers struggle teaching their students. In some cases, this could be because of full classes. On the other hand it could be because the teacher does not see the point in teaching when they get paid for being there anyways. A lot of teachers in third world countries does not really have the competence to teach either, which also cause students to drop out.
